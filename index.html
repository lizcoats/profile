About Me

I grew up in the Transylvania region of Romania (yep, Dracula’s Transylvania). Romanian people are very fond of the saying ”Never put off till tomorrow if you can do it today”. This philosophy served me well over the years, and even more so now as I transition into a new career in technology.

I started my programming journey as a self-learner tackling JavaScript as my first language. I then dove into the deep end and spent the next seven months at the Code Platoon bootcamp where I studied to become a full stack software engineer. I emerged victorious and well-versed in various programming languages, frameworks and essential development tools. I think of myself as a perpetual learner and I enjoy the dynamic nature of technology.
